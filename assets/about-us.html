About Us

The year 2020, as we know it was not just the onset of a worldwide pandemic, but also a peek into the future of small start-ups and even established businesses which both suffered the knocks and blows of financial insecurity. The tough times made businesses and professionals realize the importance of creating a strong online presence, while we recognized the urgent need of economical yet profitable online marketing encasements which could help create the plethora of possibilities that often companies are unable to find on their own. Another thing on our mind was ‘The Make in India’ movement launched by Prime Minister Narendra Modi few years back and how one could possibly contribute and help the small enterprises in India to really grow.

In December 2020, our three Co-Founders; Rishabh Sadana, Dakshesh and Ayush founded Hype Up Media with the determination and insight to bring something new to the table for everyone from Big Brands to Individual working professionals. But how is this possible? Hype Up Media doesn’t just help you grow your brand online, we actually put in the effort to understand what exactly it is you are looking for so we can accurately satisfy our customer demands. Whilst we’ve helped clients establish a good client base in order to increase sales, we’ve also worked with brands to improve their brand image, devised ways for companies to stand out against their competitors, and even provided some companies with their weak statistics that have been hindering their progress using our Brand Consultancy services. We don’t just want to grow into a big digital marketing firm, we want to help you get exactly what you want, hence the motto “Let’s grow Digital Together!”

In addition to everything else, all working professionals in our company are Generation Z, the generation that has witnessed the invention of the internet and social media growing up as well as the evolution of the world with it, so much so that we don’t just recognize the intricacies of the web but we also can’t imagine a world without them. From the necessary aesthetics that add to the visual appeal of your brand to the technical back end work that goes behind Search Engine Optimization and Search Engine Management, we offer a fresh and young frame of reference, a guaranteed quality you’ll find working with us.

We at Hype Media love what we do, and how we do it. Being three close friends that have founded the company, we keep the working environment and relationships with all our young professionals friendly and fun. The Japanese verb “to work” (hataraku) exactly translates to “to bring ease to those around you”, which is faithfully what we believe in. So that you can rest assured that your work is in happy hands!
